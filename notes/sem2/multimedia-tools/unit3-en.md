# Q1. Define Sound. Explain the Physical and Digital Characteristics of Sound Including Frequency, Amplitude, Pitch, Loudness, and Timbre.

## Introduction

Scientific point of view se sound ek mechanical wave hai jo kisi medium (air, water ya solid) ke through travel karti hai. Jab koi object vibrate karta hai, to us vibration ke karan surrounding particles me disturbance create hota hai aur ye disturbance sound wave ke form me propagate hota hai. Human ear in sound waves ko receive karta hai aur brain unhe sound ke roop me interpret karta hai.

Digital multimedia systems me sound ko electronic signals aur digital data ke form me process kiya jata hai. Isliye sound ke physical aur digital characteristics ko samajhna multimedia development ke liye bahut important hai.

## Definition of Sound

Sound ek form of energy hai jo vibrations ke karan produce hoti hai aur waves ke roop me travel karti hai. Jab ye waves human ear tak pahunchti hain, tab hum unhe sound ke roop me perceive karte hain.

Simple words me, sound vibrations ka result hota hai jo kisi medium ke through travel karti hain aur sunai deti hain.

Examples:

- Human Voice
- Musical Instruments
- Vehicle Horn
- Bird Sounds
- Environmental Sounds

Multimedia systems me sound information delivery aur entertainment dono ke liye use kiya jata hai.

## Nature of Sound

Sound longitudinal wave hoti hai. Isme particles wave ke direction ke parallel vibrate karte hain.

Sound ko travel karne ke liye medium ki zarurat hoti hai.

Sound travel kar sakti hai:

- Air me
- Water me
- Solid objects me

Vacuum me sound travel nahi kar sakti kyunki wahan particles available nahi hote.

## Physical Characteristics of Sound

Physical characteristics sound wave ki actual properties ko represent karti hain.

### 1. Frequency

Frequency sound wave ke vibrations ki speed ko represent karti hai.

Frequency batati hai ki ek second me kitni complete vibrations ya cycles produce ho rahi hain.

Frequency ki unit Hertz (Hz) hoti hai.

Formula:

Frequency = Number of Vibrations Per Second

Examples:

- Human Speech = Approximately 85 Hz to 255 Hz
- Musical Notes = Different Frequencies
- Dog Whistle = Very High Frequency

Human hearing range:

20 Hz to 20,000 Hz

### Effect of Frequency

High Frequency:

- High Pitch Sound
- Example: Whistle

Low Frequency:

- Low Pitch Sound
- Example: Drum

Frequency sound ke pitch ko determine karti hai.

## 2. Amplitude

Amplitude sound wave ki height ko represent karti hai.

Ye sound wave me available energy ko indicate karti hai.

Higher amplitude ka matlab stronger sound wave.

### Effect of Amplitude

Large Amplitude:

- Louder Sound

Small Amplitude:

- Softer Sound

Example:

- Shouting = High Amplitude
- Whispering = Low Amplitude

Amplitude sound intensity ko control karti hai.

## 3. Pitch

Pitch ek subjective characteristic hai jo sound ke high ya low hone ka perception provide karti hai.

Pitch directly frequency se related hoti hai.

### Relationship Between Pitch and Frequency

High Frequency:

- High Pitch

Low Frequency:

- Low Pitch

Examples:

High Pitch:

- Flute
- Whistle

Low Pitch:

- Bass Guitar
- Drum

Music systems me pitch ka bahut important role hota hai.

## 4. Loudness

Loudness sound ki perceived intensity hoti hai.

Ye batati hai ki sound human ear ko kitni loud ya soft feel ho rahi hai.

Loudness amplitude par depend karti hai.

Measurement Unit:

Decibel (dB)

### Examples

Approximately 20 dB

Normal Conversation:

Approximately 60 dB

Traffic Noise:

Approximately 90 dB

Aircraft Engine:

More than 120 dB

### Effect of Loudness

Higher Loudness:

- Strong Sound Perception

Lower Loudness:

- Soft Sound Perception

Human ear loudness ko relative manner me perceive karti hai.

## 5. Timbre

Timbre sound ki unique quality ya tone ko represent karta hai.

Timbre ke karan hum same pitch aur same loudness wali do sounds ko distinguish kar pate hain.

### Example

Agar Piano aur Guitar ek hi note play karein:

- Pitch Same Ho Sakti Hai

Lekin unki sound alag lagegi.

Ye difference Timbre ke karan hota hai.

### Importance of Timbre

- Voice Recognition

Timbre sound waveform aur harmonics par depend karta hai.

## WAV Audio Format

WAV ka full form **Waveform Audio File Format** hai.
